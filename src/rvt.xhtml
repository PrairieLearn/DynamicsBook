Tangential/normal basis

Consider a particle moving with position vector \(\vec{r}\) and corresponding velocity \(\vec{v}\) and acceleration \(\vec{a}\). The distance along the path is $s$.

Basis vectors:

$$\begin{aligned} \hat{t} &= \hat{v} \\ \hat{n} &= \frac{\dot{\hat{t}}}{\|\dot{\hat{t}}\|} \\ \hat{b} &= \hat{t} \times \hat{n} \end{aligned}$$
