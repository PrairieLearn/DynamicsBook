Spherical coordinates

Coordinates:

\[\begin{aligned} (r, \theta, \phi)\end{aligned}\]

To Cartesian:

\[\begin{aligned} x &= r \cos\theta \cos\phi \\ y &= r \sin\theta \cos\phi \\ z &= r \sin\phi\end{aligned}\]

From Cartesian:

\[\begin{aligned} r &= \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} \\ \theta &= \operatorname{atan2}(y, x) \\ \phi &= \operatorname{atan2}(z, r)\end{aligned}\]

Basis vectors:

\[\begin{aligned} \,\hat{r} &= \cos\theta \cos\phi \,\hat{\imath} + \sin\theta \cos\phi \,\hat{\jmath} + \sin\phi \,\hat{k} \\ \,\hat{\theta} &= - \sin\theta \cos\phi \,\hat{\imath} + \cos\theta \cos\phi \,\hat{\jmath} \\ \,\hat{\phi} &= - \cos\theta \sin\phi \,\hat{\imath} - \sin\theta \sin\phi \,\hat{\jmath} + \cos\phi \,\hat{k}\end{aligned}\]

Angular velocity:

\[\begin{aligned} \boldsymbol{\omega} &= \dot\theta \,\hat{k} = \dot\theta(\sin\phi \,\hat{r} + \cos\phi \,\hat{\phi})\end{aligned}\]

Basis vector derivatives:

\[\begin{aligned} \dot{\hat{r}} &= \boldsymbol{\omega} \times \hat{r} = \dot\theta \cos\phi \,\hat{\theta} + \dot\phi \,\hat{\phi} \\ \dot{\hat{\theta}} &= \boldsymbol{\omega} \times \hat{\theta} = - \dot\theta \cos\phi \,\hat{r} + \dot\theta \sin\phi \,\hat{\phi} \\ \dot{\hat{\phi}} &= \boldsymbol{\omega} \times \hat{\phi} = - \dot\phi \,\hat{r} - \dot\theta \sin\theta \,\hat{\theta}\end{aligned}\]

Position, velocity, and acceleration:

\[\begin{aligned} \boldsymbol{r} &= r \,\hat{r} \\ \boldsymbol{v} &= \dot{r} \,\hat{r} + r \dot\theta \cos\phi \,\hat{\theta} + r \dot\phi \,\hat{\phi} \\ \boldsymbol{a} &= (\ddot{r} - r \dot{\theta}^2 \cos^2\phi - r \dot{\phi}^2) \,\hat{r} \\ &\quad + (r \ddot\theta \cos\phi + 2 \dot{r} \dot\theta \cos\phi - 2 r \dot\theta \dot\phi \sin\phi) \,\hat{\theta} \\ &\quad + (r \ddot\phi + 2 \dot{r} \dot\phi + r \dot{\theta}^2 \sin\phi \cos\phi) \,\hat{\phi}\end{aligned}\]
